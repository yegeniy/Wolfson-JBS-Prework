Assignment for Madlibs:

ask the user for a series of words; fill in proper places in the story using the user's answers.

place holders have a ((...)) notation, here ... is the name:

example:
Our favorite language is ((a gemstone)).

program would ask you to enter "a gemstone" 

Given "Ruby", the result is:
Our favorite language is Ruby.

More advanced case:

Our favorite language is ((gem:a gemstone)). We think ((gem)) is better than ((a gemstone))

gives:

Our favorite language is Ruby. We think Ruby is better than Emerald


The interface is not specified. so it's up to me, but these two examples should work...
